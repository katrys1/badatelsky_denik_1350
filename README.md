README: Badatelský deník 1350 (Researcher's Diary 1350)
This application is a digital interactive companion for the educational program "Od oltáře do galerie" (From the Altar to the Gallery). It is designed to guide students through the process of critical visual analysis and the study of medieval iconography.
+2

📖 Program Overview
The program focuses on the Vyšebrodský Cycle, a significant Gothic panel painting from the era of Charles IV. It aims to teach students how to "read" medieval images and uncover hidden meanings.
+2


Target Group: Lower secondary school students (7th–9th grade).


Goal: To recognize the symbolism of medieval paintings through inquiry-based learning.


Authors: Johana Šebková in collaboration with Lucie Ó Súilleabháin Špalková (Cistercian Abbey Vyšší Brod).

🛠️ App Functionalities
The Streamlit application mirrors the pedagogical structure of the live program, divided into several levels:

1. Atmosphere and Orientation

Sacral Space: Simulates the environment of a monastery church to tune students into the medieval era using sensory prompts.


Architecture Quiz: Students identify the Gothic style and the orientation of the altar (typically facing East).
+1

2. Inquiry-Based Learning

Visual Analysis: Includes a timed study of the painting to encourage focused observation.


Biblical Context: Students use the Gospel of Luke (L 1, 26) to identify the central theme of the Annunciation.
+1

3. Guild Specialization (Group Tasks)
The app provides specific tracks based on the program's research groups:


Group A (Scribes): Deciphering the Latin inscription on the angel's scroll (Ave gratia plena, Dominus tecum).


Group B (Symbolists): Exploring the "Hortus Conclusus" (enclosed garden) by identifying symbolic plants and birds found in the painting.
+1


Group C (Interpretations): Focusing on the ornate garments of the angel and the gestures of the figures.

4. Progression and Evaluation
XP System: Students earn "Prestige" (XP) for correct answers and observations.

St. Luke's Guild Protocol: A final evaluation where students receive a rank—Apprentice, Journeyman, or Master—based on their research success.

🚀 Technical Setup
To run this application, ensure you have Python and Streamlit installed.

Installation:

Bash
pip install streamlit Pillow
Required Assets:

intro.mp3: Medieval background music.

poutnik.jpg / image_c6a996.jpg: Visual materials representing the traveler and the Vyšebrodský Cycle.

Launch:

Bash
streamlit run [filename].py
📚 Educational Framework
The content is based on the Framework Education Program (RVP) for Basic Education, specifically for Art Education. It utilizes the iconographic method as defined by Daniela Rywiková.
